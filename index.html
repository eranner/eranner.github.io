Bio

Eric J. Ranner

Web Developer

Honesdale, PA
570-394-9929 || eranner@gmail.com || LinkedIn

Eric was born and raised in Honesdale, Pennsylvania. He is one of six children! Being one of the middle children, for attention, Eric has always found a way to entertain people.

After graduating from Honesdale High School, he began his quest for more knowledge at East Stroudsburg University, where he majored in Hotel, Restaurant and Tourism Management. Since he grew up working in the industry, this seemed like a natural fit for him. Puting these skills to good use, while in and out of college, Eric worked at a world class resort known as Woodloch Pines. Unbeknownst to him, this is where his coding journey would begin.

After changing majors and schools, Eric seemed to find his stride at Woodloch. Entertaining guests seemed to be a fantastic use of his talents. Along the way, he actually married one of the guests there! As the old adage goes, first comes love, then comes marriage, and of course then comes the baby carriage. Eric and his beautiful wife Julia have three amazing children that bring so much joy to their sometimes very hectic lives.

So one may wonder, how did Eric become a programmer? Well, I'm glad you asked! One major aspect to Eric's job as the Corporate Social Director at Woodloch is to make many of Woodloch's entertaining games. It started with Microsoft Powerpoint, but as time went on, his skills and desires outgrew the confines of the program. Eventually leading him to teach himself how to use HTML, CSS, Javascript, and PHP.

Now, several years later, Eric holds a certificate from MiT for Full Stack Web Development. He welcomes you to try some of the fun projects that he has completed/is currently working on. Please click on the links provided and have some fun!
